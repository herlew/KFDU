VISION:TO FOSTER PEACE AND UNITY AMONG THE FULANI AND WITH THE PEOPLE OF KWARA STATE

KAUTALHO'ORE---UNITY

ABOUT US

This association is primarily created to foster unity and peace among the Fulani in kwara state. This integration will neither exclude the educated nor the non-educated Fulbe within the state. KFDU is interested in bringing together of Fulbe including the tribe nomadic counterpart. integration of Fulani herdsmen, Western educated, Eastern Literate (Islamic priests) and crop farming Fulbe to benefit from her efforts. To ensure the creation of beneficiary programs that will reach every segment of Fulbe , without marginalization in age, sex, family background, Religion factor, Economic factor and any other form of socio-cultural factor. To keep and liberate culture, norms, customs as part of our education references.
